Privacy Policy for Later is Never

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

What Data We Collect

When you save a YouTube video, the extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Video title
  • Channel name
  • Video ID
  • Thumbnail URL
  • Video URL
  • Save timestamp
  • Expiration timestamp

When you adjust settings, the extension stores:

  • Your chosen expiration period preference
  • Your sort order preference

How We Use This Data

This data is used solely to display your saved videos list and manage automatic expiration within the extension popup.

Data Storage and Sharing

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage. We do NOT:

  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Share data with third parties
  • Collect any personally identifiable information
  • Track your browsing history

Data Deletion

All stored data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension. You can also manually delete individual videos from the extension popup at any time.
